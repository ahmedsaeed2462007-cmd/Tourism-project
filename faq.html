    Bookings & Payment

    You can book a tour by following these simple steps:

    1. Choose your desired destination from the Destinations page
    2. Select the travel program that suits you
    3. Click the "Book" button
    4. Fill in your booking details
    5. Choose a payment method and complete the process

    We accept all secure payment methods:

    • Credit cards (Visa & Mastercard)
    • Bank transfer
    • Digital wallets
    • Cash on arrival (in some cases)

    Yes, our refund policy depends on the cancellation date:

    • Cancellation 30+ days before: 100% refund
    • Cancellation 15 days before: 75% refund
    • Cancellation 7 days before: 50% refund
    • Cancellation within 7 days: No refund

    Documents & Visa

    The essential documents required are:

    • Valid passport with at least 6 months remaining validity
    • Recent passport-size photo (4×6)
    • Entry visa (if required)
    • Health certificate (in some cases)

    Yes, we offer visa assistance services including:

    • Free consultations on visa requirements
    • Filling out required forms
    • Help with document preparation
    • Tracking your visa application

    For more information, contact our support team.
